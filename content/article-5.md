# How to Build and Sell AI Chatbots to Local Businesses in 2026

![AI Chatbot Dashboard](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1531482615713-2afd69097998?auto=format&fit=crop&w=800&q=80)

If you're looking for a side hustle with extremely high profit margins and almost zero ongoing maintenance, selling AI chatbots is the holy grail of 2026.

Local businesses (law firms, dental offices, plumbers) lose thousands of dollars a month because they can't answer the phone or reply to website inquiries instantly. A customer who doesn't get an immediate answer simply moves on to the next competitor.

You can solve this problem for them by deploying a custom AI assistant that knows everything about their business and works 24/7. And the best part? You don't need to know how to code.

## The No-Code AI Stack

We are going to use "no-code" platforms that let you ingest a company's data and instantly generate a chatbot.

### 1. CustomGPT.ai

**Why you need it:** CustomGPT is the industry leader for building highly accurate, hallucination-free chatbots. You just paste in the client's website URL, upload any PDF pricing menus they have, and the AI trains itself on their specific business in minutes.

You can embed this chatbot directly on their website as a widget or deploy it as an SMS responder. When a customer asks, "Do you offer emergency plumbing on weekends?", the bot answers instantly using the client's actual policies.

*   **The Business Model:** You charge the client a $1,000 setup fee, plus a $300/month "maintenance and hosting" retainer. Your actual cost is just your CustomGPT subscription.
*   **Affiliate Potential:** Up to 20% recurring commission. [Build your first bot with CustomGPT.ai.](#)

### 2. Chatbase

**Why you need it:** Chatbase is another incredibly user-friendly platform that lets you train an AI on custom data. It's fantastic for quick deployments and has a very clean user interface that clients love to interact with.

*   **Best for:** Small retail businesses, real estate agents, and e-commerce stores.
*   **Affiliate Potential:** 20% recurring commission. [Start building with Chatbase.](#)

## The "Trojan Horse" Sales Strategy

Do not cold-call businesses asking if they want to buy a chatbot. They will say no. Instead, use this strategy:

1.  **Find a Target:** Look up a local business (e.g., a roofing company) with a decent website but no live chat feature.
2.  **Build the Demo:** Go to CustomGPT or Chatbase, drop in their website URL, and build a fully functional bot for them in 5 minutes.
3.  **The Pitch:** Record a 2-minute Loom video of you interacting with *their* new bot. Ask it questions like "How much does a roof inspection cost?" and show it answering perfectly.
4.  **The Email:** Send them the video. *"Hey [Name], I noticed you didn't have 24/7 live chat on your site so I built a custom AI assistant for you using your website data. I attached a video showing how it answers customer questions instantly. Let me know if you want me to install this for you."*

Once they see their own data working perfectly, the sale is effortless.

## Scale the Agency

Once the bot is installed, it runs on autopilot. You collect the $300/mo retainer indefinitely. 

Get 10 clients using this method, and you have $3,000 in monthly recurring revenue (MRR) for a product that requires almost zero weekly maintenance. Start building today.
